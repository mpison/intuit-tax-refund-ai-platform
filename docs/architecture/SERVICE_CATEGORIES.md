# Service Categories

## Core services
Own transactional business data and domain rules.

## Identity services
Manage users, roles, clients, identity-provider administration, and security policies.

## AI assistants
Own prompts, conversation orchestration, chat memory, tool selection, guardrails, and response composition.

## MCP servers
Expose narrow, authenticated tool surfaces over business services. Do not own core business data.

## RAG services
Own document ingestion, parsing, chunking, embedding, indexing, retrieval, and source metadata.

## Model services
Own structured ML inference, feature preparation, model versions, evaluation, and fallback logic.

## Integration services
Isolate external providers and normalize their protocols and data formats.

## Platform services
Provide administrative, audit, notification, configuration, and cross-cutting operational capabilities.
