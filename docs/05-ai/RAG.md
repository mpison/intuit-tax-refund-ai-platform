# Tax Policy RAG Assistant v0.4.0

The service uses:

- Spring AI 2.0.0
- Ollama `llama3.2` for chat
- Ollama `nomic-embed-text` for embeddings
- PostgreSQL with PGVector
- Similarity retrieval followed by grounded generation

The assistant provides general policy guidance only. It does not claim access
to customer account data and does not provide legal or tax advice.
